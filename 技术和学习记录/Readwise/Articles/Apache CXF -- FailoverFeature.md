## Highlights
- The CXF Failover feature allows to configure CXF frontend clients to retry a call when the target endpoint becomes unavailable. A number of retry strategies available: a client can iterate sequentially over the alternative addresses or chose them randomly. Every strategy can be configured to do a delay between selecting the addresses.
